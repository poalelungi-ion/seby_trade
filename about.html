Our History

Founded in 2024, SEBY TRADE started with a vision to bring the purest sunflower oil from Moldova to Denmark. Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted name in the industry, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Why Choose Us?

At SEBY TRADE, we prioritize quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction above all. Our direct sourcing from local farms in Moldova ensures that you receive the finest sunflower oil every time.

We are committed to transparent business practices, reliable services, and building long-lasting partnerships. Choose SEBY TRADE for exceptional quality and service.

What We Do?

SEBY TRADE specializes in the trading and shipment of premium sunflower oil from Moldova to Denmark. We ensure the highest standards of quality, freshness, and timely delivery to meet our customers' needs.
